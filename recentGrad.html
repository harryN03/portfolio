Recent Graduate

I recently graduated with a Computer Science major degree from Toronto Metropolitan University, with a strong academic record (92.8% GPA, Dean's List for three all years I studied full-time).

Throughout my degree, I built a solid foundation across programming languages (Python, Java, JavaScript, SQL), algorithms and data structures, operating systems (with a particular focus on Linux), and mathematics - all of which sharpened my analytical thinking and problem-solving ability.

In my third year, I had the opportunity to gain 20 months of co-op experience as a Software Developer at the Ontario Public Service, where I helped maintain a production application serving 400,000+ users. That experience gave me a deep appreciation for how reliable, secure systems operate at scale - and pushed me to go further on my own.

As I moved closer to graduation, my interests shifted toward networking and cybersecurity. I completed courses in Computer Security and Networking, which drew my interest into how systems are protected, how data moves across infrastructure, and how vulnerabilities are identified and mitigated. These courses sparked a genuine curiosity in me - and ultimately shaped the direction I wanted to take my career.

Outside of coursework, I've built a home lab covering Active Directory, Windows Server, Splunk SIEM, and network configuration (VLANs, OSPF, DHCP, NAT, ACLs). I'm currently studying for my CCNA certification to deepen my networking fundamentals and am pursuing roles in Network Administration, IT Support, and Cybersecurity.
