Part-time Server

In addition to my academic and career pursuits, I've worked part-time as a Server at a local restaurant. This role has helped cover my daily expenses while building skills that translate directly into IT support and customer-facing roles.

I work in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment, managing customer orders, handling payments, and ensuring guests have a smooth experience even during the busiest shifts. Clear communication and attention to detail are essential - small mistakes can quickly snowball, so staying organized and calm under pressure is critical.

One thing I'm particularly proud of is the trust I've earned on the floor - during busy shifts, I often help lead and coordinate a team of 6, assigning tasks, supporting newer staff, and ensuring the team works smoothly together under time pressure.

While my long-term goals are in networking and cybersecurity, this experience has strengthened my communication, teamwork, and leadership skills - qualities that matter just as much in IT support and helpdesk roles, where you're constantly working with people, not just systems.
